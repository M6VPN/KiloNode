# M2.9 Known Limitations

M2.9 is a hobbyist preview readiness pass. It does not add live RF connected
mode.

Known limitations:

- No live CONNECT.
- No real connected-mode RF sessions.
- No prepared-frame promotion to the real TX queue.
- No TX dispatch.
- No RF BBS access.
- No NET/ROM.
- No forwarding or FBB forwarding.
- No LinBPQ command compatibility.
- No Mercury adapter implementation.
- No VARA or ARDOP adapter implementation.
- No FX.25 FEC or wrapping.
- No PTT or channel busy policy.
- External modem commands are read-only status commands.
- Mercury interface details remain unknown until discovery is completed.
